The Cats you will find at the cafe

Calicos
Calicos are a usual fun breed to have with their multi color patterns you cant help but want to pet them. they might even leave you with new colors on yourself! *disclaimer* Not responsible for urine or feces on clothes *disclaimer*
Tabby
We have a wide array of tabby cats many colors and variations but all of them are just as cuddly as the next with all different personalities. Beware of Jimmy....
Sphynx
We also have some more rare sphynx cats that will be dressed up for our guests to enjoy and they actaully like to be clothed! Ignore them trying to rip their clothes off.
Devon Rex
Our Devon Rex is the most playful but be careful he likes to bite!
Persian
