Activities With The Cats

Toys

Feel free to play with the cats all over the cafe we have multiple play stations with all kinds of toys.

Feeding Time

The cats have specific feeding times that we allow guests to feed them treats we will bring out.

Coffee and Tea Time

Feel free to just sit and observe the cats while you enjoy our wide selection of beverages and snacks. Some of our furry friends might even join you.
